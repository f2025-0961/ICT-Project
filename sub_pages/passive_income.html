Passive Income Opportunities

Passive income refers to earning methods where income continues over time after an initial setup, effort, or investment. Unlike skill-based or non-skill earning, passive income focuses on building assets that can generate revenue with reduced daily involvement.

Passive income is not instant and requires patience, consistency, and realistic expectations. When built ethically and strategically, it can become a sustainable long-term income source.
